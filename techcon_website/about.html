History

TechCon began in 2015 as a small meet-up of passionate tech minds in Lagos, Nigeria. What started as a modest gathering of 50 attendees has now grown into one of Africa’s most influential technology conferences, drawing thousands of participants from around the world each year. The event was created to give a voice to local developers, startups, and innovators — and over the years, it has expanded to include global tech leaders, investors, educators, and students. Through the years, TechCon has been a platform for launching groundbreaking ideas, fostering partnerships, and shaping the future of technology in Africa and beyond.

Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to connect, educate, and inspire the next generation of tech leaders. We believe in the power of technology to solve real-world problems and uplift communities. By bringing together experts and newcomers alike, we aim to create an inclusive space where knowledge is shared, ideas are challenged, and innovation thrives. TechCon promotes diversity in tech and encourages young talents—especially women and underrepresented groups—to take part in the digital revolution.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted an impressive lineup of speakers from various sectors of the tech world. These thought leaders have inspired thousands with their journeys, insights, and visions for the future. Some of our past speakers include:

  • Dr. Anita Oseni – AI Researcher and Founder of MindTech Africa
  • Kelechi Johnson – Lead Developer at Microsoft West Africa
  • Fatou Bamba – Women in Tech Advocate and Startup Mentor
  • Chidi Okoro – Cybersecurity Consultant and author of Digital Defense in Africa
Attendees also enjoy video interviews, recorded keynotes, and behind-the-scenes highlights, which are made available online after each edition of the conference.
